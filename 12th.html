    Career Path After 12th Science

    Fields
  • (For PCM students or those with a strong interest in Mathematics)
  • Computer Science Engineering (CSE)
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical/Electronics Engineering
  • Civil Engineering
  • Aeronautical/Aerospace Engineering
  • Biotechnology Engineering
  • Chemical Engineering
  • Mechatronics/Robotics Engineering
  • Artificial Intelligence and Data Science
  • Information Technology (IT)
  • Environmental Engineering
  • Petroleum Engineering
    • 2. Medical and Healthcare Fields
    (For PCB students or those interested in Biology)
  • MBBS (Doctor)
  • BDS (Dentistry)
  • BAMS (Ayurveda)
  • BHMS (Homeopathy)
  • BPT (Physiotherapy)
  • B.Sc. in Nursing
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Biotechnology/Bioinformatics
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Veterinary Science (BVSc)
  • Nutrition and Dietetics
    • 3. Interdisciplinary and Pure Sciences
    (For both PCM and PCB students interested in research or academia)
  • B.Sc. (Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics, or Biology)
  • Environmental Science
  • Microbiology
  • Genetics
  • Zoology/Botany
  • Astronomy
  • Forensic Science
  • Data Science/Statistics
  • Actuarial Science
    • 4. Technology and Emerging Fields
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • Game Development
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Cloud Computing
    • 5. Non-Technical and Creative Fields
  • Architecture (B.Arch)
  • Interior Design
  • Fashion Design
  • Product Design
  • Animation and VFX
  • Graphic Design
    • 6. Commerce and Management
    (Suitable for science students who wish to switch streams)
  • BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration)
  • Hotel Management
  • CA, CS, or CMA (Professional Courses)
  • Economics or Finance
    • 7. Government and

  • Marine Biology
  • Horticulture
  • Wildlife Conservation

    • Career Path After 12th Arts

    1. Humanities & Social Sciences
    • BA (Bachelor of Arts) in:
      • History
      • Political Science
      • Sociology
      • Psychology
      • Philosophy
      • Anthropology
      • Geography
    • Career Options: Academics, Research, Civil Services, Journalism, Social Work.
    2. Creative Fields
  • Fine Arts (BFA)
  • Performing Arts (Dance, Theatre, Music)
  • Visual Communication
  • Animation & Multimedia
  • Fashion Design (NIFT)
  • Interior Design
  • Graphic Design
  • Career Options: Artist, Designer, Animator, Performer.
    • 3. Languages and Literature
  • BA in English, Hindi, French, German, Spanish, or any regional language.
  • Career Options: Writer, Translator, Editor, Language Instructor.
    • 4. Law
  • BA LLB (5-year integrated law course)
  • Career Options: Lawyer, Legal Advisor, Judge (after further qualifications).
    • 5. Media and Journalism
  • Mass Communication
  • Journalism
  • Digital Media
  • Career Options: Journalist, News Anchor, Content Creator, Media Manager.
    • 6. Education
  • Bachelor of Education (B.Ed.) (Post-Graduation Required)
  • Career Options: Teacher, Educational Consultant, Academician.
    • 7. Event Management
  • Bachelor’s in Event Management
  • Career Options: Event Planner, Wedding Coordinator, Conference Organizer.
    • 8. Hotel Management
  • Bachelor’s in Hotel Management (BHM)
  • Career Options: Hospitality Manager, Chef, Travel Coordinator.

    • Career Path After 12th Commerce

    1. Traditional Commerce Degrees
    • BB.Com (Bachelor of Commerce) in:
      • Accounting and Finance
      • Banking and Insurance
      • Taxation
    • Career Options: Accountant, Financial Analyst, Banker.
    2. Professional Courses
  • Chartered Accountancy (CA)
  • Company Secretary (CS)
  • Cost and Management Accountancy (CMA)
  • Actuarial Science
  • Career Options: Auditor, Financial Planner, Risk Analyst.
    • 3. Management
  • Bachelor of Business Administration (BBA)
  • Bachelor of Management Studies (BMS)
  • Bachelor of International Business (BIB)
  • Career Options: Business Manager, Entrepreneur, Consultant.
    • 4. Economics
  • BA or B.Sc. in Economics
  • Career Options: Economist, Policy Analyst, Statistician.
    • 5. Banking and Finance
  • B.Com in Banking and Finance
  • Diploma in Banking and Financial Services
  • Career Options: Investment Banker, Credit Analyst, Stock Broker.
    • 6. Data Analytics & IT
  • B.Com or B.Sc. in Statistics
  • Diploma in Data Science/Analytics
  • Career Options: Data Analyst, Business Intelligence Specialist.
    • 7. Travel and Tourism
  • Bachelor of Travel and Tourism Management
  • Career Options: Travel Agent, Tour Guide, Tourism Manager.
    • 8. Insurance and Risk Management
  • Diploma in Risk Management
  • B.Com in Insurance
  • Career Options: Insurance Underwriter, Claims Manager.
