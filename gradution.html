    1. Technical
    Post Graduation Courses
  • MTech (Masters in Technology)
  • MBA (Masters of Business Administration)
  • MSc or MS (Masters of Science)
  • PGDM (Post Graduate Diploma in Management)
  • Merchant Navy Courses
  • Animation/VFX Courses
  • Entrepreneurship Courses
  • Game Designing Courses
  • PG/ Diploma Certificate Courses

    (For PCB students or those interested in Biology)
  • MBBS (Doctor)
  • BDS (Dentistry)

  • BHMS (Homeopathy)
  • BPT (Physiotherapy)
  • B.Sc. in Nursing
  • Pharmacy (B.Pharm)
  • Biotechnology/Bioinformatics
  • Occupational Therapy
  • Veterinary Science (BVSc)
  • Nutrition and Dietetics

  • Microbiology
  • Genetics
  • Zoology/Botany
  • Astronomy
  • Forensic Science
  • Data Science/Statistics
  • Actuarial Science

  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • Cybersecurity
  • Game Development
  • Blockchain Technology
  • Cloud Computing

  • Architecture (B.Arch)
  • Interior Design

  • Product Design
