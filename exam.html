    1. Science Stream Exams Engineering (PCM Students)

  • JEE Main & Advanced
  • Purpose: Admission to IITs, NITs, IIITs, and other top engineering colleges.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCM students.
  • Exam Pattern: Physics, Chemistry, Mathematics.
  • Institutes: IITs, NITs, and other top engineering institutes.
  • State-Level Engineering Exams:
  • Examples: MHT-CET (Maharashtra), WBJEE (West Bengal), KCET (Karnataka).
  • Purpose: Admission to state engineering colleges.
  • Private University Exams:
  • Examples: VITEEE (VIT), SRMJEEE (SRM), BITSAT (BITS Pilani).

    • Medical (PCB Students)

  • NEET-UG (National Eligibility cum Entrance Test)
  • Purpose: Admission to MBBS, BDS, BAMS, BHMS, and other medical courses.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCB students.
  • Exam Pattern: Physics, Chemistry, Biology.
  • Institutes: AIIMS, JIPMER, and state/central medical colleges.
  • AIIMS Nursing Exam
  • Purpose: Admission to B.Sc. Nursing at AIIMS institutes.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCB students with a focus on Biology.

    • Pure Science/Research

  • IISER Aptitude Test (IAT)
  • Purpose: Admission to BS-MS dual degree programs at IISERs.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCM/PCB students.
  • KVPY (Kishore Vaigyanik Protsahan Yojana)
  • Purpose: Scholarships and admission to research programs.
  • Eligibility: High-performing science students.
  • NEST (National Entrance Screening Test)
  • Purpose: Admission to integrated M.Sc. programs in sciences.
  • Institutes: NISER, UM-DAE CEBS.

    • Defence

  • NDA (National Defence Academy)
  • Purpose: Entry into Army, Navy, Air Force via NDA.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCM for Navy/Air Force; any stream for Army.
  • Indian Navy SSR/AA Exam
  • Purpose: Entry as sailors in the Indian Navy.
  • Eligibility: 12th PCM.

    • 2. Commerce Stream Exams

    2. Commerce Stream Exams

  • Finance and Accounting
  • CA Foundation
  • Purpose: Entry to Chartered Accountancy.
  • Eligibility: 12th Commerce or equivalent.
  • CMA Foundation
  • Purpose: Certified Management Accountant.
  • Eligibility: 12th Commerce.
  • CS Foundation
  • Purpose: Entry into Company Secretaryship.
  • Eligibility: 12th Commerce or equivalent.

    • Management

  • IPMAT (Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test)
  • Purpose: Admission to integrated BBA-MBA programs at IIMs.
  • Eligibility: 12th in any stream.
  • DU JAT (Delhi University Joint Admission Test)
  • Purpose: Admission to BMS, BBA, and other courses at Delhi University.
  • Eligibility: 12th Commerce/any stream.

    • Banking and Finance

  • CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
  • Purpose: Admission to law programs in NLUs.
  • Eligibility: 12th in any stream.
  • RBI Assistant Exam
  • Purpose: Entry-level positions in RBI.
  • Eligibility: 12th or graduation.
